Liempo Recipe

Liempo, or grilled pork belly, is a beloved Filipino dish often served during events or beach outings. It is marinated in a mixture of soy sauce, calamansi (or lemon), garlic, and seasonings before being grilled to perfection over hot coals. Whether enjoyed on special occasions or a regular day, Liempo never fails to satisfy.

Ingredients:

  • 2 lbs. pork belly
  • 1/2 cup soy sauce
  • 1 piece lemon or 3 to 4 pieces calamansi
  • 1/2 tsp ground black pepper
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 4 cloves garlic, crushed
  • 1/4 cup banana catsup
  • 1 tbsp cooking oil

Cooking Steps:

  1. Combine pork belly with soy sauce, lemon, salt, ground black pepper, and garlic. Mix well and marinate for at least 3 hours.
  2. In a bowl, pour the pork belly marinade. Add banana catsup and cooking oil to create the basting sauce.
  3. Grill the pork belly, basting the top part after flipping it over. Grill until cooked through and charred to perfection.
