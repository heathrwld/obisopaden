Mango Float Recipe

Mango float is a popular no-bake dessert from the Philippines, known for its refreshing taste and creamy texture. It is made with layers of graham crackers, mangoes, and cream, making it a delightful treat that's easy to prepare.

Ingredients:

  • 1 tablespoon of crushed graham
  • 20 pieces Graham crackers
  • 1 cup all-purpose cream or Cool Whip
  • 3/4 cup condensed milk
  • 2 cups of mangoes

Cooking Steps:

  1. In a rectangular dish, arrange 8 to 10 pieces of graham crackers. Set aside.
  2. In a bowl, combine the cream/Cool Whip and condensed milk. Mix well.
  3. On the layered crackers on the bottom, spread the milk and cream mixture.
  4. Spread out the thin sliced mangoes evenly on top of the cream.
  5. Make another layer of graham crackers; spread the cream and mango slices. You can do multiple layers if you want.
  6. Garnish the top layer with mango and sprinkle with crushed graham.
  7. Chill for at least 3 hours before serving.
