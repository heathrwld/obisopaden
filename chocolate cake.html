Chocolate Cake Recipe

Chocolate cake is a rich and delicious dessert that uses chocolate or cocoa powder as the main flavoring ingredient. It can be made in various forms such as layer cakes, sheet cakes, or cupcakes. This recipe provides a classic version that can be filled with frosting, ganache, or whipped cream and topped with additional chocolate or decorations.

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups white sugar
  • 1 ¾ cups all-purpose flour
  • ¾ cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1 ½ teaspoons baking powder
  • 1 ½ teaspoons baking soda
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 cup milk
  • ½ cup vegetable oil
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 1 cup boiling water

Cooking Steps:

  1. Gather all ingredients.
  2. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease and flour two 9-inch round baking pans.
  3. Stir sugar, flour, cocoa, baking powder, baking soda, and salt together in a large bowl.
  4. Add eggs, milk, oil, and vanilla; mix for 2 minutes on medium speed with an electric mixer.
  5. Stir in the boiling water. The batter will be thin. Pour evenly into the prepared pans.
  6. Bake in the preheated oven until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean, about 30 to 35 minutes. Cool in the pans for 10 minutes, then transfer to a wire rack to cool completely.
