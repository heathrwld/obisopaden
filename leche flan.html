Leche Flan Recipe

Leche Flan is a beloved Filipino dessert, traditionally served during celebrations such as birthdays and fiestas. This creamy custard is made from egg yolks, condensed milk, evaporated milk, and sugar, resulting in a smooth, rich texture and a sweet caramel topping. It’s a staple treat that perfectly concludes any festive meal.

Ingredients:

  • 10 pieces eggs
  • 1 can condensed milk (14 oz)
  • 1 cup fresh milk or evaporated milk
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Cooking Steps:

  1. Separate the yolks from the egg whites (only egg yolks will be used).
  2. Place the egg yolks in a large bowl and beat them using a fork or egg beater.
  3. Add the condensed milk and mix thoroughly.
  4. Pour in the fresh milk and vanilla extract. Mix well.
  5. Place the mold (llanera) on the stove over low heat.
  6. Add the granulated sugar to the mold and mix until it turns into a light brown caramel.
