Bicol Express Recipe

Bicol Express is a signature dish from the Bicol region of the Philippines, renowned for its bold, spicy flavors. The dish combines pork belly with rich coconut milk and a blend of spices, including Thai chili peppers and shrimp paste. It’s a hearty and flavorful meal that pairs perfectly with warm rice.

Ingredients:

  • 2 lbs. pork belly, sliced into strips
  • 2 cups coconut milk
  • 2 cups coconut cream
  • 1/4 cup shrimp paste or salted krill
  • 5 cloves garlic, crushed
  • 5 pieces Thai chili peppers, chopped
  • 2 thumbs ginger, minced
  • 1 piece onion, chopped
  • 2 pieces Serrano peppers, sliced
  • 1 cup water (optional)
  • 8 grams Maggi Magic Sarap

Cooking Steps:

  1. Combine pork, ginger, garlic, onion, Thai chili peppers, Serrano peppers, and coconut milk in a pan. Mix well and bring to a boil over medium heat.
  2. Reduce heat to low, cover, and simmer for 50 minutes.
  3. Add the remaining coconut cream and shrimp paste (if using). Season with Maggi Magic Sarap.
  4. Transfer to a serving plate and serve with warm rice.
