Chicken Curry Recipe

Chicken curry is a flavorful dish that originated in India and has spread across the world. It is made by simmering chicken pieces with a blend of spices and other ingredients. Now enjoyed globally, it has various adaptations, including this Filipino-style version with coconut milk, patis, and a variety of vegetables.

Ingredients:

  • 1kg chicken
  • 2 large onions
  • 3 pcs red and green bell peppers
  • 3 stalks celery
  • 3 pcs medium-sized tomatoes
  • 2 tbsp ground black pepper
  • 1 tsp MSG or granulated seasoning
  • 1 tbsp patis (fish sauce)
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 4 cloves garlic
  • 1 cup coconut milk
  • 1/2 cup water

Cooking Steps:

  1. Wash and cut the chicken into pieces and put it in a large bowl.
  2. Slice the tomatoes, carrots, and bell peppers.
  3. Heat 1/4 cup of oil in a pan and fry the potatoes until browned. Then fry the carrots and bell peppers.
  4. In the same pan, sauté onions and garlic. Add the chicken and sauté. Then, add 1 tbsp of patis and simmer for 2 minutes, covering the pan.
  5. Add black pepper, granulated seasoning, curry powder, salt, and water, then simmer for 15 minutes.
  6. Add the potatoes, celery, carrots, and coconut milk. Cover and simmer for 15 minutes until the potatoes and carrots are tender. Then, add the bell peppers and stir for 1 minute before serving.
