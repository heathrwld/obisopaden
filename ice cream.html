Ice Cream Recipe

Ice cream is a classic frozen dessert enjoyed worldwide for its creamy texture and sweet flavor. Made from milk or cream, sugar, and vanilla, it can be enjoyed in various forms such as cones, sundaes, or shakes. This recipe is simple yet delivers a delicious homemade ice cream perfect for any occasion.

Ingredients:

  • Milk
  • Sugar
  • Vanilla extract
  • Cream (optional for richer texture)

Cooking Steps:

  1. Cook the milk, cream, and sugar together until the sugar has dissolved.
  2. Transfer the mixture to a measuring cup, then add the vanilla extract. Chill overnight.
  3. Pour the mixture into an ice cream maker and churn according to the manufacturer's directions.
  4. Serve immediately or ripen in the freezer before serving.
